    This is Incredible

    I can't believe I didn't think of posting this. Kudos for the idea. Here we go...

    . rocket sauce: It's rocket sauce. Just ask Jables and Rage Kage. Any song that I like. It also happens to be my largest station.

    . sesame street: This station has anything good or bad to do with children and old classic greats such as Son of a Preacher Man, Black Dog, and Sesames Treet. The name inspires retro in my mind and of course anything I like thats related to children are on here as well. You'll even find tracks like White Unicorn on there simply because Wolfmother sounds retro.

    . daterape: Now this one I've been dying for a chance to explain. This one has anything to do with sex, rape, daterape, love, relationships, or Prison Sex. You will find of course Date Rape(the inspirational track), The Hold Steady, Queens of the Stone Age, A Perfect Circle, anything that I think has anything to do with the aforementioned.

    . intoxicated: Inspired by Billy Corgan, this has anything to do with drugs and/or alcahol. We also like to include songs by artists that we KNOW are/were on drugs. Guys like Jimi Hendrix and Scott Weiland.

    . worst band ever: This tag is dedicated to and inspired by Limp Bizkit. But Metallica also qualifies. As far as I know, those are the only 2 bands in there. That is because both bands did indeed rock at some point and one would be lying to themself if they NO LONGER liked the good stuff they did. That is why you shouldn't find any Linkin Park, Insane Clown Posse, or Evanescence in there.

    These are not by any means complete, nor will they ever be. We're always working on them and improving them. We also have been known to remove a track on the occasion. Feel free to check out the rest of them at our profile page.
